Oath ID 923: Syll (3), 279, 31-33, (inscriptional, Public, ca.334/3)


  Date: ca.334/3 (334 to 330)
  Reference type: Oath
  State: Zelea   Location: unknown
 
  Swearer:

  Swearee:
Presiding magistrate (male, mature/old, power-holder, Zeleian)
 
  Proposed by:
Presiding magistrate

Artemis    
 
  Remarks: This decree was probably set up not long after Alexander the Great passed through the area. Nine citizens were elected to act as inspectors to find out whether any public land had been occupied illegally and to impose fines. After the inspection was carried out, eleven citizens were to be elected from among the citizens who did not possess any public land. Eight of the eleven would serve as judges of those accused of owning public land illegally, and the remaining three would serve as advocates.
The text indicates that "that judges and advocates were to swear by Artemis according to the law" (ὀμόσαι δὲ τοὺς δικαστὰς κ̣αὶ τοὺς συνηγόρους τὴν Ἄρτεμιν κατὰ τὸν νόμον).
For the oath of the advocates see oath id 924.
