  Remarks: This is an agreement between Megara (see below) and Selinus. This entry takes into account textual changes in SEG 29.403. Fragment h, which has been placed at the top left of the text begins with the word hόρκια in larger script than the rest of the text. This is perhaps the heading of the text? Fragment def, lines 5-6 indicates that the agreement is to be ratified by oaths. It is a matter of debate as to whether this text is an agreement concerning the admittance of exiles from Megara Hyblaia to Selinus, or concerning exiles from Selinus in Megara Hyblaia (or perhaps even Megara Nicaea).
