  Remarks: This inscription is a first century copy of a sixth century document. The last line of the original text survives in text A.
The inscription records honours awarded to Medikes and the sons of Aesop and their descendants. The text indicates that the demos formalised these honours by swearing oaths. The text reads. "with regard to these things the demos cut oaths" (καὶ ἐπὶ τούτοισιν δῆμος ὅρκιον ἔταμον).
