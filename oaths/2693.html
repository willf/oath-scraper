  Linguistic: omos
 
  God(s) invoked:
unknown    
 
  Remarks: This passage refers to a generic oath, not a specific one. It is required in cases where couples are failing in their procreative duties. Female inspectors visit the houses and try to prevent the couples from mistakes and folly. If they fail, they report to the law-guardians who themselves attempt the same thing. If they also are unable to influence whichever party of the couple is failing in their duties, they must inform the state-council and post up in writing this sworn statement. The unreformed party then has recourse to a trial, and if s/he loses, penalties are inflicted- in particular disqualification from attending various feasts and celebrations.
