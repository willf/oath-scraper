Oath ID 575: IPArk, 15, 53-72, (inscriptional, Public, 360-350)


  Date: 360 to 350
  Reference type: Oath
  State: Orchomenos   Location: unknown
 
  Swearer:
Aristanor (male, mature/old, power-holder, Euaemnian)
Onomantus (male, mature/old, power-holder, Euaemnian)
Laeas (male, mature/old, power-holder, Euaemnian)
Saocles (male, mature/old, power-holder, Euaemnian)
  Swearee:
Representatives (male, mature/old, power-holder, Orchomenian)
 
  Proposed by:
unknown
 
  If taken: The synoecism would be formalised
  If refused: unknown
  If kept: Many good things
  If broken: Destruction of self and descendants
 
  Taken: yes   True: unknown
  Impact: unknown
  Consequences of breach: unknown
 
  Sanctifying features unknown
 
  Statement: [That they would be guileless in the synoecism with the Orchomenioi with regard to the agreement, by Zeus Ares, by Athena Areia, by Enyalios Ares. That they would never move away from the Orchomenians, not by Zeus Ares, not by Athena Areia, not by Enyalios Ares]
ἀψευ[δ]ήων ἂ<ν> τὰν συϜοι[κί]α̣ν τοῖς Ἐρχομινίο[ι]ς πὸς τὰς συνθέ[σι]ς, νεὶ τὸν Δία τὸν Ἄρ[η]α̣, νεὶ τὰν Ἀθάναν τ̣ὰν Ἄρειαν, νεὶ τὸν [Ἰ]νυάλιον τὸν Ἄρηα̣, [ο]ὐδ’ ἂν ἀνισταίμαν ἀπὺ τοῖς Ἐρχομιν[ί]οις οὔποτε, οὐ τὸν [Δ]ία τὸν Ἄρηα, οὐ τὰ̣ν Ἀθάναν τὰν Ἄρε̣[ι]α̣ν, οὐ τὸν Ἰνυάλιον τὸν Ἄρηα· κεὐορκέντι μὲν τἀγαθά̣, [ἐ]πιορκέντι δὲ ἐξο̣[λέ]σθαι αὐτὸγ καὶ γένος.
 
  Linguistic: nei tan, nei ton, ork, wmos
 
  God(s) invoked:
Zeus    
Athena    
Enyalios    
 
  Remarks: This text refers to the synoecism of Orchomenos and Euaemon.
The text was originally published as IG V, 2 343.
The four swearers are presuambly representatives of the community of Euaemon.
For the corresponding oath sworn by all the citizens of Orchomenos see oath id 576.
