  Statement: [That every year each member of the tribe of the Neuri is turned into a wolf and after remaining so for a few days returns to his former shape]
Λέγονται γὰρ ὑπὸ Σκυθέων καὶ Ἑλλήνων τῶν ἐν τῇ Σκυθικῇ κατοικημένων ὡς ἔτεος ἑκάστου ἅπαξ τῶν Νευρῶν ἕκαστος λύκος γίνεται ἡμέρας ὀλίγας καὶ αὖτις ὀπίσω ἐς τὠυτὸ κατίσταται
 
  Linguistic: omnu
 
  God(s) invoked:
 
  Remarks: Herodotus writes that Greeks in Scythia told him that every year each member of the tribe of the Neuri is turned into a wolf and after remaining so for a few days returns to his former shape. He notes that those who say these things do not persuade him, but they say it nevertheless and swear oaths that it is true.
