Oath ID 1597: Asclepiades, FGrH #12, FrGH #12, (literary, Myth., unknown)


  Date: mythical time
  Reference type: Oath
  State: Argos   Location: unknown
 
  Swearer:
Amphiaraus (male, mature, free, Argive)
  Swearee:
Adrastus (male, mature, power-holder, Argive)
 
  Proposed by:
Adrastus
 
  If taken: Good relations between Amphiaraus and Adrastus would be ensured
  If refused: Good relations between Amphiaraus and Adrastus would not be ensured
  If kept: unknown
  If broken: unknown
 
  Taken: yes   True: yes
  Impact: Ultimately leads to Amphiaraus' part in the expedition against Thebes
  Consequences of breach: n/a
 
  Sanctifying features unknown
 
  Statement: [That any differences between himself and Adrastus would be arbitrated by Eriphyle (wife of Amphiaraus and sister of Adrastus)]
Ἀμφιάραος ὁ Ἰο-
κλέους
γήμας Ἐριφύλην
τὴν Ταλάου καὶ διενεχθεὶς ὑπέρ τινων πρὸς Ἄδραστον,
καὶ πάλιν διαλυθεὶς ὁρκούμενος ὡμολόγησεν ὑπὲρ ὧν
ἂν διαφέρωνται πρὸς ἀλλήλους αὐτός τε καὶ Ἄδραστος
ἐπιτρέψειν Ἐριφύλην κρίνειν καὶ πείθεσθαι αὐτῇ.
 
  Linguistic: ork
 
  God(s) invoked:
unknown    
 
  Remarks: Although Amphiaraus knew that the expedition against Thebes was doomed, and was unwilling to take part, he was bound to go by this oath. Eriphyle, who had been bribed by Polyneices with the necklace of Harmonia, decided that Amphiaraus should go, and he was forced to comply with her wishes.
