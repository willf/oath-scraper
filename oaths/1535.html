  Statement: [? That they would abide by the following terms:
1. The peace was to last for thirty years
2. Athens was to give up Nisaea, Pagae, Troezen and Achaea
3. Neither side was to make an armed attack on the other
4. Disputes were to be submitted to arbitration
5. Each side was to keep what it possessed at the date of the conclusion of the treaty
6. Lists of the allies on each side were included
7. Any state not listed (except Argos) could ally itself with either side
8. Athens could establish friendly relations (but not an alliance) with Argos]

unknown    
 
  Remarks: Thucydides states that the first gounds for war against Athens which the Corinthians had was the fact the Athenians had fought against them in a time of truce.
