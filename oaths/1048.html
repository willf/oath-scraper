  Remarks: This law provided that no land or houses could be handed over to a board of mnemones in the year of Apollonides (which was either about to start, or had started recently). If anyone wished to bring a suit about land or houses within a period of 18 months, existing procedures would apply whereby the jurors would swear that "what the mnemones know shall be binding". After this period of 18 months has expired, those who held property in the year of Apollonides are to be regarded as the legal owners, and if their title is disputed they are only required to substantiate their claim by an oath.
For this oath see oath id 1049. The text also stipulates that any Halicarnassian who abides by this law may bring a case, according to the oath taken and agreements recorded in the temple of Apollo.
