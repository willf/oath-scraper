Oath ID 994: IC IV, 75, Frag A, lines 8-12, (inscriptional, Public, 480-450)


  Date: 480 to 450
  Reference type: Oath
  State: Gortyn   Location: unknown
 
  Swearer:
The pledgee of land (male, mature, free, Gortynian)
Witnesses (male, any adult age, free, Gortynian)
  Swearee:
The pledger of land (male, any adult age, free, Gortynian)
 
  Proposed by:
unknown
 
  If taken: If believed he can keep the land
  If refused: He cannot keep the land
  If kept: unknown
  If broken: unknown
 
  Taken: n/a   True:
  Impact: unknown
  Consequences of breach: unknown
 
  Sanctifying features unknown
 
  Statement: [That the land in question is the property of the pledgee and not that of the pledger]
ὀμνύμεν δὲ ε῏ μὰν τούτο μ[έν ἐστι ἀβλοπίαι δικαίος πρὶν] μ̣ολέθαι τ[ὰν δίκαν, ο῏ δ’ ἐνεκύρακσαν μὲ ἔμεν - - -]
 
  Linguistic: omnu
 
  God(s) invoked:
unknown    
 
  Remarks: The text indicates that during disputes over pledged property, the person to whom property was pledged should summon the pledger in order to mark off the land (i.e. measure it), and if the one summoned does not appear the pledgee should mark off the property himself, and warn the pledger to appear in the agora in four days time before two witnesses. They are to swear that the land in question is the property of the pledgee and not that of the pledger.
For the oath of the pledger see oath id 996. For an almost identical text see oath id 984 and 986.
