  Remarks: This inscription refers to the return of exiles to Mytilene as ordered by Alexander the Great.
The text is fragmentary, but we can determine that 20 arbiters were to be elected by the demos to facilitate the reconciliation at Mytilene. 10 men were to be chosen from the returning exiles and 10 from those who had remained in the city. These arbiters were to decide what to do with the money set aside for the implementation and "the oath which the citizens are to swear" (καὶ περὶ ὄρκω [τὸγ κε ἀπομόσσοισι οἱ] πόλιται·). This was presumably a new oath of allegiance.
The arbiters were then to put their proposals before the people, who would then deliberate.
