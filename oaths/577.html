  Statement: [That they would be guileless in the synoecism with the Euaemnians with regard to the agreement, by Zeus Ares, by Athena Areia, by Enyalios Ares. That they would never drive away the Euaemnians, not by Zeus Ares, not by Athena Areia, not by Enyalios Ares]

  Remarks: The text indicates that in the future the people of the newly synoecised community of Orchomenos will renew their oaths (see oath id 575 and 576), and that the oath ceremonies (τὰ ὅρκια) will now be the same, whereas previously the citizens of the two communities swore different oaths. However, the citizens who were formerly from the city of Euaemon will be able to swear their old oath and will not be compelled to swear the oath of the Orchomenians.
