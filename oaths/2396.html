  Statement: [That Cyrus found those they were looking for thanks to the guidance of the Hyrcanian defectors from the Assyrian alliance]
Πῶς δὲ Κῦρος, ἔφη, καὶ οἱ σὺν αὐτῷ ἐφ’ οὓς ἐπορεύοντο; Ὅτι νὴ Δί’, ἔφη, ἀκούω ἀφεστηκότας τῶν πολεμίων Ὑρκανίους τινὰς καὶ ἐλθόντας δεῦρο οἴχεσθαι ἡγουμένους αὐτῷ.
 
  Linguistic: nh

Zeus    
 
  Remarks: Cyaxares wakes up from a drunken night and is furious to find that Cyrus and the rest of the Median troops, apart from his fellow-revellers, have left. He orders them to be fetched back, Cyrus if he is willing but the Medes in any case. The cavalry sent to carry out these orders are at a loss for some time, and though they eventually find some guides, they do not reach Cyrus' camp until about midnight the next night.
