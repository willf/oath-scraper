  Statement: [That the swearer does not want lentil seeds]
μή μοι φακούς, μὰ τὸν Δί’, οὐ γὰρ ἥδομαι·
 
  Linguistic: ma ton
 
  God(s) invoked:
Zeus    
 
  Remarks: The " φακούς" seem to refer to lentil seeds as a crudité rather than "lentil soup". The verb for consuming soup is rhophein, whereas here we have (en)tragein which normally refers to things that one chews or nibbles. In line 2, we see that this food is being served not as a meal, but as an accompaniment for drinks. Cf. Solon fr. 38 where people are said to take bread, cakes or lentils with their wine.
