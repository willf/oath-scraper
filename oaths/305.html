unknown    
 
  Remarks: This inscription records an alliance between Athens and Chios.
The Athenian oath was sworn before the Chians who had come to Athens as presbeis. The terms of the alliance indicate that the Athenians and Chians would come to the aid of each other to the best of their ability if they were attacked by a third party. These oaths were stated to have been in addition to and not in violation of those sworn in the King's Peace (see oath id 328) which is described as a peace and oath and agreement.
For the corresponding oath of the Chians see oath id 306. IG II(2) 35 is a copy of IG II(2) 34
