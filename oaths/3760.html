  Remarks: This inscription records a treaty between Naupactus and Messene.
The text is too fragmentary to be fully restored. It is clear that the Locrians, the Messenians and a group described as the "-atidae" (or "-atides") swore the oath before the oath officials known as the "horkisteroi". A group of officials known as the "iduoi" were to ensure that the oath was sworn or those not swearing were punished. If they failed to do so, they would pay a fine.
Those who refused to swear the oath were to pay a fine. The staters were to be sacred to Athena i.e. paid into the treasury of Athena Polias.
Those who break their oath were to be cursed by Athena Polias, their property confiscated, and to set up "hammered statues".
