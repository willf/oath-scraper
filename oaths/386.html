Oath ID 386: Aristophanes, Frogs, 177, (literary, Comic., 405)


  Date: 405
  Reference type: Oath
  State: Underworld   Location: Edge of Charon's lake
 
  Swearer:
Corpse (male, mature, n/a, n/a)
  Swearee:
Dionysus (male, mature, power-holder, n/a)
 
  Proposed by:
n/a
 
  If taken: n/a
  If refused: n/a
  If kept: He will remain dead
  If broken: He will come back to life
 
  Taken: yes   True: yes
  Impact: Xanthias relents and agrees to go to Hades with Dionysus
  Consequences of breach: n/a
 
  Sanctifying features none
 
  Statement: [That he will not accept nine obols for agreeing to carry the luggage]
ΔΙ. Λάβ’ ἐννέ’ ὀβολούς.
ΝΕ. ᾿Αναβιοίην νυν πάλιν.
 
  Linguistic: self-curse
 
  God(s) invoked:
unknown    
 
  Remarks: This is a conditional self-curse, unique in that it is uttered by a corpse, and is the antithesis of what a living person would say (i.e. "May I not live if I do X" vel sim.).
